Final Product Flowability Improvement

The Challenge

Final product exhibited poor flowability in railcars due to residual moisture — causing customer complaints and transport delays.

Our Approach

Training, Education & Preparedness

Environmental Compliance Training

Midwestern cement facility

The Challenge

Repeated minor violations due to staff misunderstanding of pH and metal effluent limits — with regulatory fines and loss of certification looming.

Our Approach

Hands-on training in sampling techniques, compliance reporting, and chemistry fundamentals, with mock audits and live feedback over a two-week period — plus rewritten Standard Operating Procedures in accessible language for all shift operators.

Results

0Violations for two quarters following training
2Quarters running, and counting
  • On-site lab procedures certified for internal use
  • Internal staff now self-auditing for proactive response
